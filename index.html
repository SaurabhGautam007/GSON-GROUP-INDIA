Our Journey

Two Decades of Milestones

2005

Foundation

Gson Group founded with a vision to build a diversified business house. First office opened in Mumbai with a team of 12.

2009

IT Division Launch

Gson Technologies incorporated. First enterprise IT contract secured with a leading Indian bank, generating ₹50Cr in revenue.

2013

Manufacturing Entry

Acquired first manufacturing plant in Pune. Launched Gson Manufacturing with automotive component production for Tier-1 OEMs.

2017

Global Expansion

Opened offices in Dubai, Singapore, and London. Gson Capital established with first $200M fund targeting Southeast Asian growth companies.

2021

Billion Dollar Milestone

Crossed $1B in consolidated annual revenue. Listed Gson Technologies on BSE. Headcount surpassed 5,000 globally.

2026

Next Chapter

Entering AI infrastructure, green manufacturing, and digital trade corridors. $2.4B revenue. 8,500 employees. 40+ countries. The journey accelerates.
